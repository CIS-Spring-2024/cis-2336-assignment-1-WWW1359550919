About

Owner of the Tianliang cafeteria

Weiheng Chen

With unwavering dedication and a profound passion for the culinary realm,

Weiheng has dedicated years of relentless commitment at Tianliang Catering Management Co., Ltd.

Embracing the ethos of "green catering," he ardently champions its promotion, ceaselessly delving

into research and development encompassing distinctiveness,health enhancement, and uncompromising quality.

Today, Tianliang Catering stands poised upon a new paradigm, emphasizing industrialization and internationalization.

As the culinary domain continues its ascent, Weiheng envisions a future marked by accelerated growth and prosperity for the industry.

Firmly convinced that through tireless endeavors and leveraging food as a conduit, an increasing number of

patrons will come to cherish the essence of Chinese cuisine,paving the way for its resplendent

journey towards triumph.Mission: To beckon talents from across the globe, uniting in the creation of a culinary saga that

transcends borders and ignites a legend in catering.
